An Australian singer

And my idol!

Background Information:

Rosé is a New Zealand and South Korean singer and songwriter. Born in New Zealand and raised in Australia, she moved to South Korea and signed with label YG Entertainment following a successful audition in 2012. She rose to prominence as a member of the South Korean girl group Blackpink, which debuted in August 2016 and became one of the best-selling girl groups of all time.
