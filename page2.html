What I like about her:

Blackpink’s Rosé (Roseanne Park) is known for her gentle, thoughtful, and, at times, shy personality, which contrasts with her powerful, emotive stage persona. Often identified as an ENFP, she is considered highly empathetic, artistic, and emotionally driven, with a deep passion for music, creativity, and a quiet, domestic life outside of the spotlight.
