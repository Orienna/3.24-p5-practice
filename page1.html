Her self-written debut album!

"rosie" is the debut studio album by BLACKPINK’s Rosé, released on December 6, 2024, via The Black Label and Atlantic Records. The 12-track project is a self-written, deeply personal exploration of her early twenties, including themes of fame and heartbreak, highlighted by the hit single "APT." with Bruno Mars.

Key Details About "rosie":

Release Date: December 6, 2024.

Tracklist & Themes: The album is noted for its raw, vulnerable, and personal songwriting, covering topics like heartbreak and personal struggles. The tracklist includes "Number One Girl" and "Toxic Till the End".

Production: Rosé co-produced the album, featuring collaborations with Bruno Mars, Carter Lang, and Omer Fedi.

Chart Performance: Upon release, the album debuted at No. 3 on the Billboard 200 and became a top-five album in the UK, with 316,000 equivalent units sold globally in its first day.

Reception: It has been described as a heartfelt, intimate look into her life, though some critics found it less revealing than expected.
